# Frequently Asked Questions

**Does my design come soldered?** \
If you use only through-hole parts, then it'll be soldered and assembled for you here at HQ! If you use any SMD parts however, you'll have to solder it yourself.

**What can I make my case out of?** \
You can make it either out of 3D printed parts, laser cut acrylic, or both!

**What's the thickness/colour of the acrylic sheets?** \
3mm thick, clear

**Where are the parts shipped from?** \
Vermont, USA

**Can I use X part?** \
Please check the approved parts list [here](/parts)

**What colours are the keycaps?** \
You'll get a random mix of white and red caps!
